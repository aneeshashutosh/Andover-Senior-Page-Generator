Tutorial

Drag images into each cell to add them to your senior page.
You can drag images to change their position in the cell.
Double click a cell to change the background to white to create a letterbox.
Double click it again to reset it to the default.

The top bar holds all tools you'll need to create the perfect senior page.
The first icon will save your image and download it; press it when you're done!
The next ones affect the layout of your page, changing the shape, size, and letterbox locations.
Like before, you can select multiple letterbox styles. Try out the settings for yourself!

Your senior page is ready!
